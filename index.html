Interesting facts about camels

Interesting facts about pigeons
  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. They can also recognise each letter of the human alphabet,differentiate

4. TEDDY BEAR

Click here to view the steps.

Interesting facts about teddy bears.
  1. Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot
    a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  2. The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
    She created a soft plush with movable arms and legs and a friendly
    face after taking her nephew to the zoo in 1903.
  3. The world's smallest stitched teddy bear is a mere 0.29 inches tall!
    It was created by South African artist Cheryl Moss who is known for her
    minuscule work.

6. FLYING CICADA

Click here to view the steps.

Interesting facts about flying cicadas.
  1. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs. They are about the size of
    a grain of rice when they drop from a
    tree branch to the ground and start digging.
  2. They improve lawns by digging tunnels that allow air into the soil.
    When they die, their rotting bodies put nitrogen into the soil.
  3. Most have red-orange eyes. But occasionally cicadas have blue, white
    or grayish eyes.
